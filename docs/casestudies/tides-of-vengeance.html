Concept / Pitch

Tides of Vengeance

An original pitch recognised by a lead designer at DBGA. A world built from the sea floor up.

Concept

Tides of Vengeance is an original game concept with a fully developed pitch and worldbuilding documentation. The project was recognised by a lead designer at the academy as a standout original concept — praised for its internal consistency and the distinctiveness of its fictional world.

Worldbuilding Approach

The world of Tides of Vengeance was built around a single guiding question: what does revenge look like when the thing you're avenging no longer exists? Every faction, location, and mechanic was designed to refract that question differently.

Documents Produced
  • Original pitch document — concept, tone, core loop, target audience
  • Worldbuilding documentation — factions, geography, mythology
  • Narrative design notes — protagonist arc, thematic pillars
