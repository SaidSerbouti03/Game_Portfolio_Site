Coming Back

Every thousand years, the god Mucco grants one cow the right to return. You are a hamburger. You are going home.

Overview

Coming Back is a shoot 'em up developed in five days for a game jam on the theme of Origins. The concept: a divine bovine mythology in which the god Mucco periodically resurrects a fallen cow — in the form of a hamburger — and grants them a chance to ascend back through a shoot 'em up gauntlet inspired by Cuphead and Cho Aniki.

The premise is absurdist, but the execution is earnest — which is what makes it work.

My Contribution
  • Original concept and narrative premise
  • All animated background GIFs — produced and integrated in 5 days
  • Original music composition and implementation
  • Creative direction for the team's AI image and visual tool pipeline — no dedicated artist on the team

"No artist on the team. Five days. A playable, visually coherent game. That's what problem-solving under pressure looks like."

Creative Direction Under Constraint

The absence of a dedicated artist forced a fast pivot to AI-assisted visual production — but the outputs still needed creative direction. I developed a prompt documentation workflow on the fly: consistent tone references, style constraints, and iteration notes that let the team generate coherent visuals without a single visual specialist.

This is the most transferable skill to come out of Coming Back: knowing how to maintain aesthetic coherence when the usual pipeline doesn't exist.
